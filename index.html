Mini cookies filled with nutella

Mini cookies filled with Nutella are bite-sized treats featuring a soft, golden-brown exterior that crumbles gently. Each cookie encases a rich, creamy center of Nutella, offering a delightful combination of buttery sweetness and the decadent flavors of chocolate and hazelnut. Their petite size makes them perfect for snacking, with a tempting aroma that invites you to savor every delicious bite.

These mini cookies are a delightful treat, featuring a soft, buttery dough that’s filled with creamy Nutella. To make them, you'll need all-purpose flour, unsalted butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, baking soda, and salt. Start by creaming the butter and sugars, then mix in the eggs and vanilla. Combine with the dry ingredients, chill the dough for 30 minutes, and then scoop, flatten, and fill with Nutella. Seal the dough and bake until golden.

Ingredients: ½ cups all-purpose flour ½ cup unsalted butter, softened ¼ cup granulated sugar ¼ cup brown sugar, packed 1 large egg 1 teaspoon vanilla extract ½ teaspoon baking soda ¼ teaspoon salt Nutella (about ½ cup for filling)

steps

  1. 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour ½ cup unsalted butter, softened ¼ cup granulated sugar ¼ cup brown sugar, packed 1 large egg 1 teaspoon vanilla extract ½ teaspoon baking soda ¼ teaspoon salt Nutella (about ½ cup for filling)
  2. Cream butter and sugars, then mix in egg and vanilla. Combine with dry ingredients. Chill dough for 30 minutes. Scoop, flatten, fill with Nutella, and seal. Bake until golden and enjoy!

Mini Vanilla Cupcakes Ingredients All-purpose flour Baking powder Baking soda Salt Unsalted butter Granulated sugar Eggs Vanilla extract Buttermilk For the Frosting: Unsalted butter Powdered sugar Vanilla extract Milk Sprinkles (optional, for decoration)

  • Mini Vanilla Cupcakes Ingredients 1 cup all-purpose flour ½ teaspoon baking powder ½ teaspoon baking soda ¼ teaspoon salt ½ cup unsalted butter, softened ¾ cup granulated sugar 2 large eggs 2 teaspoons vanilla extract ½ cup buttermilk
  • For the Frosting: ½ cup unsalted butter, softened 2 cups powdered sugar 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 2 tablespoons milk Sprinkles (optional, for decoration)

Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven and line a mini muffin tin with cupcake liners. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Cream Butter and Sugar: In a separate bowl, cream together the softened unsalted butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add Eggs and Vanilla: Beat in the eggs, one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract until well combined. Combine Ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the buttermilk, and mix until just combined. Fill Muffin Tin: Spoon the batter into the prepared mini muffin tin, filling each liner about two-thirds full. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 10-12 minutes. Cool: Remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Make the Frosting: In a bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy, then gradually add powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and milk, mixing until smooth. Frost the Cupcakes: Once the cupcakes are completely cool, frost them with the prepared vanilla frosting and add sprinkles if desired
