Mini Cookies Filled with Nutella

These mini cookies are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth! I love making them because they’re easy to prepare and the Nutella center adds a delicious surprise. They're great for sharing at parties or enjoying on a cozy night in.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • Nutella (about ½ cup for filling)

Steps:

  1. Cream butter and sugars, then mix in egg and vanilla.
  2. Combine with dry ingredients.
  3. Chill dough for 30 minutes.
  4. Scoop, flatten, fill with Nutella, and seal.
  5. Bake until golden and enjoy!

Mini Cakes Filled with Cookie Dough

I chose these mini cakes because they blend two of my favorites: chocolate cake and cookie dough! They’re perfect for celebrations or a fun treat at home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup mini chocolate chips
  • ½ cup buttermilk

For the Cookie Dough Filling:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup mini chocolate chips

For the Frosting:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Mini chocolate chips for garnish

Mini Cupcakes

These mini vanilla cupcakes are a hit at any gathering! They’re light, fluffy, and the perfect treat to enjoy with friends.I love sharing with my friends.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ½ cup buttermilk

For the Frosting:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Sprinkles (optional, for decoration)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven and line a mini muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients, alternating with buttermilk.
  6. Spoon batter into the muffin tin, filling each liner two-thirds full.
  7. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean, about 10-12 minutes.
  8. Cool, then frost and add sprinkles if desired!
